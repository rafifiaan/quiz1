Popular Food

Rujak Cingur

This dish consists almost like pecel, but contains more fruit such as sour mango, jicama with a mixture of black shrimp paste called petis and peanut sauce.

Semanggi Surabaya

The unique one is eat this special dish using Kerupuk -deep fried crackers made from starch- not using spoon or fork.

Rawon

Well-known as Black Soup. Eat with steamed rice and shrimp crackers is the best way to enjoy this dish.

Lontong Kupang

Kind of sea shell that is small in size between the seeds of rice and soybean seeds, and rice cakes with paste and lime dressing

Tahu Tek / Telor

A simple dish consisting mixed of fried tofu, scrambled egg, sprouts, potato, cucumber, and poured with peanut black shrimp paste sauce served with shrimp cracker.

Sate Klopo

Satay, known as "sate" in Bahasa, has diverse regional varieties, with Surabaya's specialty featuring shredded coconut-covered beef served alongside peanut sauce, sweet soy, rice cake, shallots, and bird's eye chili.
