Transportation

Suroboyo Bus

Bus transportation services with modern facilities owned by the Surabaya City Government which are intended as public transportation similar to city buses in the Surabaya City metropolitan area

Suroboyo bus has two routes, namely the north-south route and the west-east route. The north-south routes include (Purabaya Terminal-Jalan Rajawali pp) and the west-east route (Unesa Lidah-ITS Sukolilo pp).

Start operating hours : 06.00 - 22.00 WIB

Route Maps : Check Route

WiraWiri Feeder Surabaya

Feeder transportation services that have lines and/or tariffs that are integrated with existing rapid mass transportation modes in the city of Surabaya. Just like Suroboyo Bus, this service is managed directly by the Surabaya City Government through the UPT Public Transportation Management unit.

As is known, the WiraWiri Suroboyo feeder transport started launching and operating on Thursday (3/3/2023). In initial operations, this land transportation served five travel routes.

Start operating hours : 05.30 - 21.30 WIB
